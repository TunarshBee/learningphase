How to make bread

Bread is a starchy food made from flour, water, and yeast mixed together and baked in an oven. I like bread because it takes hours to digest

List of all the ingredients needed to make bread

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of yeast
  • 1 cup of whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup off all purpose flour + additional for kneading in/dusting
